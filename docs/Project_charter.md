- Higher average basket value
- Personalized customer experience

Benefits for the environment:

- Reduced food waste through planned purchases
- Healthier and more structured eating habits

## 5. THE PURPOSE AND THE OBJECTIVES OF THE PROJECT:

The purpose of the project is to design and implement a smart online grocery shopping
platform which combines e-commerce platform, meal planning and personalized
recommendation features.


Objectives:

- To integrate a functional meal recommender system
- To develop a meal planning module linked to product purchasing
- To increase the sales of products in the online store
- To deliver a stable, tested and documented software solution
- Increase the likelihood of a recurring customer

## 6.5 DESCRIPTION OF THE FINISHED PRODUCT, SERVICE OR RESULT

Finished product will be an online grocery store with built in recommendations for meal
planning. The website will have user accounts, to help us build recommendations, meal
planning calendar and automatic shopping list generation. The recommendation system will
work with the help of implicit and explicit user preferences. To ensure the collection explicit
user preferences – we will allow them to grade meals, between 1 and 5.
